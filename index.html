Debarpan Bhattacharya

I am a MTech(Research) student at Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore, India. Before joining at IISc, I spent amazing 4 years at Jadavpur University, Kolkata pursuing my bachelor's in Electrical Engineering.

At IISc, I am associated with LEAP lab under the guidance of the coolest :) Prof. Sriram Ganapathy. I've been working on project COSWARA, one of the prominent responses of IISc to limit the spread of COVID-19. It shows that COVID-19 can be effectively screened using respiratory sounds like cough, breathing and speech based rapid diagnosis [project repo]. Check out our publications for more details.

During my bachelor's, I have worked on multiple signal processing based projects with sensing and healthcare applications. I was fortunate to receive close mentorship from Prof. Sugata Munshi, Prof. Biswajit Bhattacharyya and Prof. Sudip Misra.
